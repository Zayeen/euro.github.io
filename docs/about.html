Mission Statement

Securing the Connected Future

Welcome to EURO IoT Intelligence.

We are a dedicated team of security researchers, ethical hackers, and privacy advocates. Our mission is simple: to expose the vulnerabilities in the Internet of Things (IoT) ecosystem and champion the adoption of robust encryption standards.

Why We Exist

The "smart home" promise is one of convenience, but it has often come at the cost of privacy. As our homes become filled with listening, watching, and sensing devices, the line between helpful assistance and intrusive surveillance blurs. We believe you shouldn't have to sacrifice your privacy to enjoy the benefits of modern technology.

Our Core Principles

  • Zero-Trust Architecture: Assume every device is compromised until proven otherwise.
  • Local-First Processing: Data should be processed on the device, not in the cloud.
  • Open Standards: We support open-source protocols like Matter and Thread that allow for transparency and auditability.

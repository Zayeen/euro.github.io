About The IoT Lab

Welcome to The IoT Lab. This is a digital playground where we break things so you don't have to.

The Internet of Things is messy. It's a fragmented world of different protocols, insecure devices, and brilliant possibilities. Here, we document the experiments, the failures, and the successes in building a truly smart, secure home.

What We Explore

  • Home Labs: Running Home Assistant, Node-RED, and custom Zigbee networks.
  • Security: VLANs, firewalls, and keeping "smart" fridges away from banking data.
  • Hardware: Teardowns, flashing custom firmware (ESPHome/Tasmota), and soldering iron accidents.

The Philosophy

We believe technology should be:

  • Local First: Cloud-free whenever possible.
  • Private: Your data stays in your house.
  • Open Source: If you can't hack it, you don't own it.

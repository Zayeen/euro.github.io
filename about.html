About The IoT Lab

Breaking smart homes so you don't have to.

Mission

The Internet of Things is messy. We document the experiments, failures, and successes in building a truly smart, secure home.

Philosophy

We believe technology should be:

  • Local First: Cloud-free whenever possible.
  • Private: Your data stays in your house.
  • Open Source: If you can't hack it, you don't own it.

Lab Stats

  • Sensors: 45+ Zigbee Nodes
  • Network: 3 VLANs, Unifi Stack
  • Server: Proxmox Cluster (3 Nodes)
  • Uptime: 99.9% (mostly)

Focus Areas

  • Home Assistant & Node-RED
  • Network Security & VLANs
  • Custom Hardware (ESP32)
  • Protocol Analysis (Matter, Thread)

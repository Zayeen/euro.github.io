About the Lab

ConnectedCasa is a testing ground for the future of private, local, and secure smart home technology.

Mission

Democratizing Secure Automation

The Internet of Things is currently a mess of fragmented ecosystems and privacy concerns. Our mission is to document the failures and successes of building a truly smart home that doesn't compromise on security or local control.

We break things in our lab so you can build a stable, resilient system in yours.

Philosophy
  • • Local First: Cloud-free whenever possible for maximum reliability.
  • • Privacy: Your home's data should never leave your four walls.
  • • Open Source: If you can't inspect the code, you don't truly own it.
Focus Areas

Ecosystems

Home Assistant, Node-RED, and ESPHome are our primary tools for orchestration.

Focus Areas

Protocols

Deep dives into Matter, Thread, Zigbee, and Z-Wave Long Range reliability.

Focus Areas

Security

Network isolation, VLAN strategies, and hardware-level hardening.
